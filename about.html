The Lore

The Lore of Valorant Protocol begins on a future Earth forever changed by the First Light event, which introduced Radianite to the world, a mysterious element that grants extraordinary abilities to certain individuals.

This event split the world, creating the Radiant individuals and unleashing global conflicts. The organization, Kingdom, sought to control Radianite.

The Valorant Protocol was formed from special operatives to protect the world. Agents are deployed to stabilize any crisis. Explore our archives to understand the history, the technology, and the heroes of this fractured world.
